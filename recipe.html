Recipe

Chocolate cake


"This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. this version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kids will enjoy it.!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour : 80 g

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smoooth dought

4

Bake in the oven
